## Rate Limits (per tenant)

- Baseline: **600 requests/minute** (burst up to **60 rps**)
- Headers: `X-RateLimit-Limit`, `X-RateLimit-Remaining`, `Retry-After`

## Retries

- Use **exponential backoff** on `429` or retryable `5xx` (start at 250ms, cap at 5s, max 6 tries).
- For non-idempotent endpoints: always include `Idempotency-Key`.

## SLA (Platform targets)

- **Availability:** 99.5% monthly (standard tier)
- **RTO:** ≤ 4 hours
- **RPO:** ≤ 15 minutes (MongoDB Atlas backups + PITR)
- Incident comms via status page & email.

<Callout type="info">
  Higher tiers can be agreed commercially. Contact Arcube for tenant-specific SLAs.
</Callout>
