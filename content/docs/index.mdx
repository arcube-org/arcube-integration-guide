Welcome to the **Arcube Product Definition & Integration Guide**. This documentation will help you understand Arcube's product and integrate Arcube's ancillary services into your systems.

## Overview

Arcube is a cutting-edge third-party ancillary services platform designed to help travel businesses - including airlines, Travel Management Companies (TMCs), and travel agencies - unlock new revenue streams and enhance the passenger experience.

We have spent 3+ years aggregating dozens of ancillaries from hundreds of suppliers with extensive global coverage, so that travel providers don't have to. Arcube uses AI to dynamically personalize offers based on real-time trip context such as destination, travel dates, duration, local events, and weather - no recommendation logic to build or maintain on your side.

## Integration Methods

Arcube offers four integration methods. These are designed to be flexible - choose the option that best fits your engagement strategy, or use all four concurrently for maximum impact.

## Choosing the Right Method

There is no **better** or **worse** integration method - the more integration points a tenant implements, the more conversion opportunities they are likely to generate. The real question is **what is technically feasible** based on your technology stack, platform limitations, and integration capabilities.

| Method | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Widget** | Embedding offers inside the booking flow |
| **Email** | Post-booking engagement before departure |
| **API** | Full control at any touchpoint in your system |
| **White-Label POS** | A dedicated branded checkout experience |

Arcube supports a spectrum of integration approaches - from simple to advanced - ensuring every tenant can find the right fit for their technical environment and business objectives.
